Life Sports Foundation

 

Our Mission is the enhancement of life "through" Sports.

Life Sports Foundation is dedicated to helping people of all abilities who are facing physical, situational, or medical challenges to enrich their lives through sports. We provide opportunities for enhanced athletic lifestyles through endurance events, specialized equipment, training, competition expenses and education.  We also fulfill our mission by supporting programs and other non-profit organizations who help others live their lives to the fullest. We believe that ALL Lives are made better through healthy exercise and event comradery.

Become a Sponsored Athlete - Life Sports Foundation provides challenged athletes with funds to help with entry fees and needed equipment. We can also provide free entries into our Triathlon, Running, and Cycling events. Please contact us to apply.

Race for a Reason - Life Sports Foundation holds 6 Triathlons, 3 runs, and 2 Cycling events each year. These events encourage a healthy lifestyle for adults and children, provide a platform to raise funds for Life Sports Foundation and our charity partners encouraging donations and fundraising.
